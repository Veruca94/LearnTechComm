1. **Instructional Documentation** - User guides, training manuals, and how-to materials
2. **Technical Reports** - Research findings, data analysis, and scientific documentation
3. **Policy & Procedure Documents** - Organizational frameworks for compliance and operations
4. **White Papers** - Authoritative reports and thought leadership content
5. **Grants & Proposals** - Funding applications and persuasive proposals
6. **API Documentation** - Developer-focused guides with code samples and integration instructions
7. **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)** - Step-by-step instructions for critical processes
8. **Marketing & Promotional Content** - Technical marketing materials and brand communications
9. **Legal Documents** - Legal technical writing and compliance documentation
